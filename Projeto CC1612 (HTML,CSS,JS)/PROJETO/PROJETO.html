For more than 40 years, Activision has been changing the way people play. In the process, we built one of the largest portfolios of recognized brands and today we are one of the most valuable interactive entertainment companies in the world.

Founded in 1979 as the first independent developer and distributor of video game software, the company launched a series of several million unit titles sold in the early 1980s for the Atari 2600, including Pitfall, Kaboom! and River Raid. And since then, Activision has created some of the most iconic and memorable gaming experiences ever. Today, the company is a world-leading developer, publisher and distributor of interactive entertainment and leisure products for various consoles, portable platforms and PCs.

Over the past five years, we have continued to thrive as a leading provider of digital entertainment, whose games attract a variety of players.
